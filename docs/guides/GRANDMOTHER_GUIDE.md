# LIMEN OS — The Grandmother's Guide

Hello! If you're reading this, you're looking at a new kind of computer system. We call it **LIMEN OS**, but you can just think of it as a helpful partner that lives in your home.

We designed this using the **"Washing Machine Test"**: it should be as easy to use as your laundry dial, but as smart as a library.

---

## 1. How do I talk to it?
You don't need a keyboard or a mouse. Just talk to it like you're talking to a friend.
- **Start by saying:** "Hey Limen..."
- **Ask for things:** "...open my photos" or "...what is the weather?"
- **If it's too loud:** "...be quiet" or "...calm down."

---

## 2. What if it doesn't understand me?
Sometimes computers get confused, just like people. 
- If Limen doesn't get it right the first time, it might ask, "Did you mean...?"
- If you get frustrated and say **"Forget it!"** or **"Give me the keyboard!"**, the system will automatically step back and show you a simple menu you can touch or type on. 
- **We never want you to feel stuck.**

---

## 3. The "Look" of the system
The system can change its clothes (we call these **Paradigms**).
- If you like things **Classic** (like an old typewriter or early computer), just say: "Make it look classic."
- If you want it **Calm** and quiet, say: "Switch to calm mode."
- It can even sense if you're tired or stressed and might offer to dim the lights or simplify the screen for you.

---

## 4. Why is this different?
Most computers expect you to learn *their* language. **Limen OS tries to learn yours.**
It's built to be a #-year partner—something that grows with you, knows your routines, and stays out of your way when you just want to get things done.

---

## 5. Summary for the Grandkids
- **No Typing Required:** Use your voice or just point at the screen.
- **Always Safe:** It's designed to never leave you at a "dead end."
- **Friendly:** It's more like a toaster or a washing machine than a complicated PC.
